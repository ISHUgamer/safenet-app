Latest Cyber Threats

High Risk
AI Voice Phishing (Vishing)
Scammers use AI to clone voices of family members to demand urgent money transfers. Always verify via callback.
High Risk
UPI Fake QR Code Scam
Fraudsters share QR codes that deduct money instead of sending it. Never scan unknown QR codes to receive money.
Medium Risk
Fake Job Offer Messages
WhatsApp messages offering high-paying part-time jobs that ask for a small "registration fee" upfront — it's a scam.
Stay Alert
Public Wi-Fi Data Theft
Avoid banking or sharing personal info on public Wi-Fi. Use mobile data or a VPN instead.

Safety Tips For You

🔒
Use Strong PasswordsMix letters, numbers and symbols. Never reuse passwords across apps.
📱
Enable Two-Factor AuthenticationAdd an extra layer of security beyond just a password.
👁️
Check App PermissionsDeny camera, mic, or location access to apps that don't need it.
🔄
Keep Apps UpdatedUpdates patch security vulnerabilities. Enable auto-update on your phone.
⏰
Limit Screen TimeSet daily limits on social media. Take breaks every 45 minutes.
